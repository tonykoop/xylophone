# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A finished concert-style chromatic xylophone photographed as a real physical object: a graduated fan of 25 rectangular flat-bottom hardwood bars in warm reddish-orange African Padauk, descending smoothly from the longest bar on the left to the shortest on the right, every bar the same constant width and thickness so the eye reads a clean tapering wedge of identical-section blanks. The bars rest on a light-toned hard-maple trestle frame — two parallel suspension rails carried on four square legs at standing-play height — and each bar is suspended on thin black 1/8-inch paracord threaded through small holes near the two vibration nodes, so the bars float a hair above the rails rather than touching them. Soft round-over on every long bar edge catches the light. Two hard-rubber mallets with slender shafts rest across the keyboard end. Clean neutral mid-grey studio background, soft directional studio light, three-quarter product-photography angle from slightly above the right, ~50mm lens, shallow but coherent focus, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels, no measurement callouts.

## In-Context Prompt

The same Padauk-and-maple chromatic xylophone staged in a bright woodworking shop, frame standing on a real plank floor, a player's two hands holding hard-rubber mallets mid-strike over the lower bars, warm natural window light raking across the orange-red bar fan and pale maple frame. Shavings and a hand plane sit softly out of focus on a bench behind. Shallow depth of field isolating the struck bar, environmental storytelling of a one-session shop build, concept visualization only, photorealistic, no text, no dimension annotations.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of a single suspended Padauk bar at one of its node holes: a small drilled hole near the bar end with thin black paracord passing cleanly through it, the bar floating just clear of the pale maple suspension rail, the chamfered hole edge and the fine grain and quartersawn figure of the reddish-orange wood crisp under hard raking sidelight. Tight focus on the cord-through-hole suspension geometry and the rounded-over bar edge, blurred neighboring bars receding behind. High detail, photorealistic, no measurements, no text.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, or fake maker marks; no dimension lines, callouts, or rulers; no parabolic arch undercut on the bar undersides (these are flat-bottom bars); no metal resonator tubes under the bars; no impossible suspension; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
