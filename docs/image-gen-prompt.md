# Xylophone Image-Gen Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority. Do not infer bar
lengths, node-hole positions, resonator lengths, or frame joinery from these
prompts.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a finished compact 25-bar chromatic xylophone, C5 to C7 concept, with African Padauk flat rectangular bars arranged in two chromatic rows on a hard-maple trestle frame. Show paracord-style suspension passing through node holes, Baltic-birch corner gussets, a clean 21-inch-class tabletop frame proportion, and hard rubber mallets laid beside the instrument for scale. The bars should be reddish-orange Padauk with satin finish, no arch undercuts, no mandatory resonator tubes, and a crisp educational/prototype build aesthetic. Clean studio background, soft directional lighting, 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop-performance scene of the compact xylophone on a sturdy bench or low stand while a player uses hard rubber mallets. Include a tuner, pilot bar offcut, small drill jig, and neatly organized bars in the background to suggest the measured-tuning workflow. Natural shop light, warm wood tones, realistic mallet grip, clear chromatic bar layout without printed note names.

## Detail / Macro Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of several Padauk xylophone bars on cord suspension: flat-bottom rectangular bars, smooth rounded edges, clean node holes with cord passing through, and a hard-maple rail below. Emphasize wood grain, slight color variation between bars, precise spacing, and the tactile contact surface where hard mallets strike. High-detail macro, shallow depth of field.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, marimba arch undercuts, oversized resonator forest, fake note labels, impossible cord paths, extra hands, or distorted mallets.
