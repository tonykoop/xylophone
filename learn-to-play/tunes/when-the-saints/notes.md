# Notes: When the Saints Go Marching In

**Provenance:** Traditional African-American spiritual, earliest documented recording 1896.
Published in various PD hymnals before 1925. Melody is in the public domain.
The famous Louis Armstrong recording (1938) is NOT PD — use only the lead-sheet melody.

**Range check:** C5–A5. The A5 appears only once (bar 14, pickup to final phrase).

**Practice tips:**
1. The melody repeats almost exactly — once you know bars 1–8, you know the whole piece.
2. The "march" character comes from strong quarter-note beats — don't swing the eighths.
3. This tune works well as a finale in a percussion ensemble — play it twice through.
