# Simple Gifts — Songsheet

**Key:** F major  **Tempo:** ♩= 100  **Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★☆
**Range:** F4–D5  **Source:** Elder Joseph Brackett, Shaker hymn (1848), PD

*(In K:F, b = Bb automatically)*

## Notes
- "Simple Gifts" has a smooth, flowing character — connect each note.
- The Bb (written as b in K:F) is the only non-C-major note.
- This was Aaron Copland's source for the "Appalachian Spring" variations (1944) — his
  arrangement is copyrighted, but Brackett's original melody is PD.
