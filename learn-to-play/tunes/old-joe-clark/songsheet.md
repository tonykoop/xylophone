# Old Joe Clark — Songsheet

**Key:** A mixolydian (A major with G natural)  **Tempo:** ♩= 132
**Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★☆  **Range:** D4–F5
**Source:** Traditional Appalachian fiddle tune, public domain

## Modal note
A mixolydian = A major with a lowered 7th degree (G natural instead of G#).
The G-natural gives old-time Appalachian fiddle tunes their characteristic "open" sound.

## Notes
- This is the fastest tune in the songbook — start at ♩= 80 and build up.
- The repeated-note figures (A2 A2 A2) drive the rhythm — keep them even.
- Two sections: A part (A-A-G feel) and B part (higher e-e-e run).
