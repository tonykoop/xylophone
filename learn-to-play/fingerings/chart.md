## Mallet technique
- Hard rubber or plastic mallets вҖ” wooden mallets create a softer, more muted tone.
- Strike near center; resonators extend below each bar.
- Xylophone bars are thicker than glockenspiel вҖ” a firmer stroke is needed.
- The sound decays quickly (shorter than glockenspiel) вҖ” use staccato for clean passages.
